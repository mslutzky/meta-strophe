THE STRUCTURE OF AFFECT

A map of the regulatory sphere
Sphere · Field only

The sphere maps thirteen affect states as structural locations in regulatory space. Each reflects a combination of valence, demand, and peptide supply.

Threat compresses the field toward speed. Opportunity opens the recalibrative middle.

Click any affect name on the sphere to learn more about it.

See Instructions for additional guidance.

How to use the sphere
  • Threat / Opportunity — switch between the two hemispheres.
  • Fixed View / Rotate — lock the sphere or drag to explore in 3D.
  • Click any affect name — opens its address, derivation, function, and clinical note.
  • 2D Maps — compare flattened hemisphere views side by side.
  • Advanced — seams, boundaries, comfort width, staged build, and clinical examples.
